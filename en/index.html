About us

The Intervir studio is an independent animation collective.

Our animations emerge from the dialogue between art, technology, and resistance, exploring social, historical, and human themes with a critical and poetic voice.

We work with Free and Open Source Software out of ethical and aesthetic conviction: we believe that the freedom of tools is inseparable from the freedom to create.

Intervir means telling stories that disobey, imagining possible futures, and creating space for other ways of seeing and acting in the world.
